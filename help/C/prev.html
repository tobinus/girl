It is possible to record from supported radio stations in GIRL if you have installed the streamripper application.

If you have installed streamripper on your computer, then you can record audio broadcasted from any of the supported radio stations.

If you select the radio station from "Stations" and click on "Listen" in the GIRL program after you listened to a radio station previously, and then click on "Record", the radio station will be recorded and the recorded audio file will be stored in your home directory.
